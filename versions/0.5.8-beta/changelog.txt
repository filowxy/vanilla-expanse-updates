Vanilla Expense 0.5.8-beta

## What's new
- Initial beta release of Vanilla Expense modpack (NeoForge 1.21.1)

## Performance
- Tuned JVM parameters for ZGC (SoftMaxHeapSize=3072m, 4G heap)
- Disabled entity shadows and asynchronous chunk writes
- Configured Sodium, C2ME, Lithium, FerriteCore, ModernFix, Embeddium
- Reduced particle count and render distance

## Mods
- 200+ mods for gameplay, performance, and visuals
- Includes World-Scape (custom terrain generation)
- See modlist.txt in the modpack root for the full mod list
